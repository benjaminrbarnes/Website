Slovenia

Part 1

For spring break 2016, I solotraveled Slovenia. The first few days were spent in Bled and the rest of the trip was in the capital, Ljubljana. Part 1 has pictures from Bled, the surrounding area, and Vintgar Gorge. Part 2 features Ljubljana, the Škocjan Caves, and Predjama Castle.

Beekeeping is one of Slovenia's oldest traditions. The colored panels help orient the bees and make it easier for the beekeeper to identify individual homes.
