Receipt Calculator

Download via Google Play
I built this app over the course of a day or two, solely for the convenience of my roomates and I. We were tired of having to divide up the grocery store receipt with pen and paper as some items only 3 people would be in on while 5 were in on another. I created a version in Python originally but decided to create an Android version to have on the go. Currently the only Android Application that I have published to the Google Play Store.

Soundboards

Download FG via Dropbox -- Download AT via Dropbox

Building these soundboards was my first intoduction to Android Developement and Java. Over winter beak (early 2014) while visiting home I decided to challenge myself to lean Android. There was a sharp learning curve for me as I had never worked with Java or XML which slowed down the development process. I created the Family Guy one originally and because the code was so polymorphic, I transfered it into an Adventure Time soundboard. I utilized a grid layout which allowed the app to automatically resize, taking care of tablet optimization for me. The apps were originally on the Play Store before being taken down. You can download the APKs above via Dropbox.
